Welcome

Welcome to the website of Future Hope People!

We are a nonprofit organization that was founded in Ghana in 2003. Since December 2004 we are also registered in Germany under the name Future Hope People Deutschland e.V.

Our goals are the promotion and execution of development projects, organization of sponsorships for children, support of schools and working together with volunteers on-site in Ghana.

Please find more details about who we are, what we do and how you can help on the following pages.
